## CONTRADICTION DETECTED

> **Client Statement**: "The last payment of ₹8,00,000 was made in **February 2026**."

> **Payment Receipt (Receipt No. GVH-2026-0892)**: Shows payment date as **14 March 2026**.

**Discrepancy**: 1-month difference between verbal claim (February 2026) and documentary evidence (March 2026).

**Legal Implication**: The discrepancy does not affect the delay claim (possession was due December 2023, and final payment in either Feb or March 2026 is still well after the possession date). However, the inconsistency in the client's own statement could be exploited by opposing counsel to question overall credibility.

**Risk Level**: Medium — Must be addressed proactively in the complaint filing.

**Recommended Action**: Acknowledge the discrepancy in the complaint, state the receipt date (March 14, 2026) as the authoritative date, and explain the client's verbal statement was an approximation.
